How GhanaArtisan Works

A transparent, verified platform connecting Ghana's skilled workforce with clients across all 16 regions.

Simple 4-Step Process

Whether you're hiring or offering services

1

Register & Verify

Create your account and upload a valid Ghana ID card. Our admin team verifies your identity within 24 hours.

2

Build Your Profile

Add your skills, experience, service category, and set your rate. Upload credentials and certificates.

3

Get Hired

Clients search by region and skill. You receive job requests directly and can accept or decline.

4

Get Paid

Receive secure payments via Paystack — Ghana's leading payment platform — directly to your account.

Who Is It For?

Artisans

  • Register and get ID-verified
  • List your skills & set your rate
  • Receive job requests from clients
  • Accept or decline requests
  • Get paid securely via Paystack
  • Build reviews & grow your business

Clients

  • Create a free client account
  • Search artisans by region & skill
  • View verified profiles & ratings
  • Send service requests directly
  • Pay securely after job completion
  • Leave ratings and reviews

ID Verification

Every artisan on GhanaArtisan is verified using a valid Ghana government-issued ID. This ensures trust, safety, and accountability for both clients and artisans.
